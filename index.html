EXERCISE, DIETS, EQUIPMENTS AT IT'S BEST


General Importance of Exercising Daily

Exercise helps people loose weight and lower the risk of some diseases and also helps a person age well.

Importance of Cardio vascular Exercise

Reduces blood pressure,increase in insulin sensitivity,good cholestrol,increases exercise tolerance.

When to and When not to exercise

When you are really sore, really stressed,sleep deprived,feeling under the weather don't exercise. The best time of the day to exercise is a time you can stick with.

Drinking Water before and after Exercising

Drink 17 to 20 ounces of water 2 to 3 hrs before you satrt exercising. Drink 8 ounces of water 20 to 30 mins before you start exercising or during warm-up. Drink 7 to 10 ounces of water every 10 to 20 mins every 10 to 20 mins during exercise. Drink 8 ounces of water no more than 30 mins after you exercise.

Tips and Tricks to know When Undergoing the fitness journey

1- Try to diet and flow a meal plan to avoid gaining back lost fat.
2- Always make sure to engage your core and glutes when doing the intensive part of an exercise.
3- Always try to maintain the proper form during exercising.
